
[[Font scales in Data visualisation Style guides]]

20 > 14 > 12 > 10 > 8 ... 4 > 2 > 1 ... 0.75 > 0.5

- 20 - Descriptive chart title
- 14 - Chart subtitle/chart name
- 12/10 - detail in chart
- 12/10 - ticks/axes
- 4 - focus lines 
  - 16 markers?
- 2 - lines
- 1 - chart element lines
- 0.75 - chart axis
- 0.5 - chart grid
